Our Strategic Pillars

We focus on long-term, sustainable impact through three core pillars.

Legislation & Advocacy

Promoting inclusive policies and advocating for the removal of taxes on menstrual products to make them more affordable.

Coordination

Creating platforms where girls and stakeholders can actively participate in decision-making processes.

Investment

Supporting local production and advocating for dedicated funding for menstrual health initiatives.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Together, we can ensure that no girl is left behind because of menstruation.
